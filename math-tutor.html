The Role

You do not need to be a master teacher

You are given login access to the same learning software your students use — so you can see exactly what each student is working on, where they are excelling, and where they are stuck. For every exercise assigned, you receive the fully worked solution.

Your job is to guide, not lecture. You work through specific problems the student is stuck on, using the material already in front of you. A senior faculty member is always available for consultation. You are never on your own.
