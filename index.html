Students willing to practise daily, participate actively, and commit to the full program. This is for families who are serious about their child's academic future — and we honour that with equally serious preparation.

A voluntary donation of $150 goes entirely toward student prizes and end-of-program recognition. No student will be turned away due to inability to donate.

Students who show outstanding attendance, diligence, and improvement will be recognised with prizes at our closing ceremony on August 30th.
